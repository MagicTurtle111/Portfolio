Ingredients for my favourite recipe

  • Hoisin sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken thighs
  • Broccoli
  • Basmatic Rice
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Honey
  • Green and Red chilles

The recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 celsius
  2. Cut the broccoli into florets(small trees) and put it in a bowl
  3. In the bowl add chicken thighs and season with salt and pepper
  4. In another bowl mix together the honey, hoisin sauce, soy sauce and minced garlic
  5. Add this marinade to the mixing bowl and stir well
  6. Spread the chicken and brocolli on a baking tray and put it in the middle of your pre heated oven
